||THE CREAK||

Superbly arranged and tightly executed: The Creak is custom-made indie folk from Oakland, CA. Their exceptional songwriting creates music full of memorable melodies, beautiful harmonies, and deft instrumental interplay, all while maintaining a sound all their own. Previous playing engagements have included sets at The Great American Music Hall, Bottom of the Hill, various festivals, and shows with great acts such as Shakey Graves and Hot Buttered Rum.

"...it's this sense of weirdness peppered through the Creak's agile newgrass that makes the band equal parts intriguing and appealing....and that shows in their effortlessly complex arrangements and their crisp unity." (No Depression)
